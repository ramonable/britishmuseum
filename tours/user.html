This is the shortest tour and encompasses an extraodinary presentation dedicated to the act of preserving objects, artworks, and images.

Enjoy the experience of the most popular artifacts of the whole Museum – consisting of 8 exhibits.

Explore the British Museum’s most famous treasures with an illuminating 40-minute guided tour. Discover the Rosetta Stone, the Lewis Chessmen and the Parthenon sculptures, as well as some lesser-known but fascinating objects, with expert guidance that will take you to the heart of the Museum’s collection. The tour is for adults, but children under 12 are welcome (when accompanied by an adult). The tour will visit a number of galleries on different floors of the Museum and involves a lot of walking.

Discover The British Museum's selection of works of art representing different cultures and time periods and encourages visitors to explore the Museum on their own.
